Install this app
  1. Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots, top right of your browser)
  2. Tap Install app or Add to Home screen
  3. Confirm by tapping Install / Add

Wording varies slightly by browser (Chrome, Samsung Internet, etc.), but every Android browser offers one of these two options in its menu.

Install on iPhone
  1. Tap the Share icon in Safari's toolbar
  2. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  3. Tap Add in the top right

The app icon will appear on your home screen and opens full-screen, offline, just like a native app.

Counselling Aids

Pre-Contemplation / Contemplation Stage
  1. Relevance — Why will quitting benefit you?
  2. Risks — Is smoking harmful to you?
  3. Rewards — What are the benefits of quitting? (health, money, stamina)
  4. Roadblocks — What is preventing you from quitting?
  5. Repetition — Repeat motivation next visit
Preparation / Action Stage
  1. Reinforce the benefits of quitting
  2. Tell about medication options: NRT and non-NRT
    1. Duration of treatment 12 weeks
    2. High chances of quitting with medication
  3. Set: quit date
  4. Tell: Family members, friends & co-workers
  5. Anticipate:
    1. challenges & triggers
    2. withdrawal symptoms and how to overcome them
  6. Remove: get rid of tobacco related products (lighter, ashtray)

Reference

Quick References

Fagerstrom Test — Cigarettes

Question 1

How soon after you wake up do you smoke your first cigarette?

Question 2

Do you find it difficult not to smoke in places where you should not, such as church, school, cinema, library, bus or hospital?

Question 3

Which cigarette would you most hate to give up?

Question 4

How many cigarettes do you smoke every day?

Question 5

Do you smoke more during the first few hours after waking, or during the rest of the day?

Question 6

Do you still smoke if you are sick or ill and in bed most of the day?
